About Us關於我們

Racing Dynamics is a newly business development unit of Racing Electric Instrument co. ltd. The ISMS policy of REI is applied here. Racing Dynamics是一家由六俊電機儀具公司所成立的新事業開發部。 我們與六俊遵守著同樣的資通安全政策。

We are founded on November 2022. With the primary focus on unmanned aircraft system integration. 我們成立於2022年11月，目前專注在無人機系統的整合。

Our business can mainly be classified into 2 categories, the domestic division and the foreign division. 本開發部大致上可分兩個部分，本土分部和國外分部。

Racing Electric Instrument co. ltd六俊電機儀具股份有限公司

Together with the affiliated unicorn companies, we intend to utilize our capabilities of rapid prototyping and small scale production in aerospace defense industry. 本土分部致力於和獨角獸公司同盟合作，期待能把我們在航空產業中快速打樣與小規模生產的優勢發揮到最大。
